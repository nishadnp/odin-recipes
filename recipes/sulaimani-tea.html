Sulaimani Tea

A special malabar tea to complement heavy meal

Believed to have originated in the Arab world, Sulaimani tea is a spiced tea popular in Malabar region of India. With its delectable sweet-sour taste, refreshing and digestive properties, it is perfect after a heavy meal.

Savour it after a heavy meal or in a monsoon day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp ginger grated
  • 2 nos cloves
  • 4 nos 1/4 inch cinnamon
  • 2 nos green cardamom
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp black tea dust
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice

Directions

  1. Add the water to a saucepan and bring to a boil, adding the spices. Boil for a minute or two.
  2. Add tea dust and sugar (as required). Boil until the tea is strong and somewhat dark.
  3. Turn off the flame. Add the lemon juice, close and keep for a minute.
  4. Then open and strain into a tea pot. Serve Hot!
